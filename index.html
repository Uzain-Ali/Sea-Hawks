Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common DUI defense questions

How can a DUI Attorney help me?

A DUI attorney can protect your rights, challenge evidence, negotiate with prosecutors, and potentially reduce or dismiss charges. They understand the complexities of DUI law and can navigate the legal system on your behalf.

What are the penalties for a DUI in Seattle?

Penalties can include license suspension, fines, jail time, mandatory alcohol education programs, and installation of an ignition interlock device. The severity depends on factors like BAC level and prior offenses.

What happens after I'm charged with a DUI?

After being charged, you'll have an arraignment where you enter a plea. Then there may be pre-trial motions, negotiations, and possibly a trial. It's crucial to have an attorney guide you through each step.

Will I lose my driver's license?

Depending on the circumstances, your license may be suspended. However, an attorney can often help you request a hearing to challenge the suspension or obtain a restricted license for essential driving needs.
